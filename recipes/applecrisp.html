Ingredients

  • 9 medium sized green apples
  • 1/2 cup of flour
  • 1/2 cup of brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup of oil
  • 2 cup of cranberries, preferrably canned
  • 1 tsp of cinnamon
  • A dash of nutmeg

Instructions

  1. Peel your apples and cut them into quarter
  2. Combine your flour, brown sugar, oil, cannamon, and nutmeg in a medium sized bowl
  3. Place your apples in a pan and cover them evenly with cranberries
  4. Cover your apples with the contents of you bowl
  5. Place in the oven and bake for 1 hour at 350 degrees
